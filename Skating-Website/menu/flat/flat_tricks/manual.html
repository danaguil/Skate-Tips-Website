How To Manual

Manuel Skateboard GIFfrom Manuel GIFs

This trick will help improve your balance on your board, good warm-up trick as well.

Practice

This is a trick that you will need to practice every time you skate, very fun and easy trick that requires minimal effort, no pop and scoop. I've been riding for 1+ year and I still practice them.

MAIN TIP: Focus on balance through controlled weight distribution, adjust through small movement

Try to start rolling slowly and lift into a manual for short distances. Gradually increase the distance as you improve.

Fakie

Might feel weird at first but just do your normal ollie and lean towards your board. Not difficult unless you're really confortable on your board.

Nollie

Get confortable just giving small pops on your nose, as if you're trying to do an nollie. The most difficult part would be sliding your back foot. Sliding the foot is going to level it out for nollies.

Switch

Everything is the same for a switch ollie, the foot placement and the technique but in your switch position. Getting comfortable riding switch is the first step for this trick.
