-- Shove-It --

**We will have both FS and BS shove it in this page**

How To BS Shove-It

This video explain perfectly on how to shove-it!!

Now this is a trick where your both foots are going to be leaving your board, so committing will be the real challenge.

Here's a slow motion shove it. It's all in the scoop when it comes to a shove-it.

Practice first stationary(not moving) before rolling in order to learn it quickly.

Practice

Remember to jump forward when scopping. After you scoop your front foot will catch first, following your backfoot. Tip: learn it on grass/carpet, less fear and easier to land one.

Fakie

MAIN TIP: Can start by going very slow when doing your fakie shove-it then progress your speed through the days.

This trick can be very easy than regular shove for some people, so experiment and see what works for you!!

Nollie

Same technique as a shove it and fakie shove it, start off slow then increase your speed. Because this is nollie, you will need to move to land it, unless you want to try a switch shove. Nollie can also be very easy for you, so try it out.

Switch

Your board will be going all over the place for this trick, so feel out what foot position is best for you. You can try stationary for this, then gain speed to land one.

Ensure that you are scopping and giving good pressure to not get the board to flip and in order to complete a full rotation.

How To FS Shove-It

This video explain perfectly on how to FS shove-it!!

One of my favorite tricks to do because they will look nice if done properly.

Practice

Remember to jump backwards when popping. After you pop your front foot will catch first, following your backfoot. Tip: learn it on grass/carpet, less fear and easier to land one. There's not much of a scoop in this trick, but a little pop, could be tricky, but unless you give a little pop and really give it a shove, it's look good

MAIN TIP: If you have you front foot hanging out a bit more and in the middle, then you will get a bigger pop. You can increase the height by how lower you have your front foot. Test where your foot needs to be.

Fakie

One can give a little scoop with Fakie FS Shove and I will say it way easier than regular FS Shove it. You can make it look 'lazy' if you're good at fakie.

This trick can be very easy than regular FS Shove for some people, so experiment and see what works for you!!

Nollie

Same technique as a FS Shove it and fakie FS Shove it, you just really gotta focus on that shove motion to get it's full rotation and remember to jump back and commmit.

Switch

Going to be a bit tricky to get this one good, but as long you give it a good pop you'll be good to go.
