-- Ollie --

How To Ollie

This video explain perfectly on how to ollie!!

It doesn't matter how clean your ollie looks just yet, but if you want to perfect them, then do THIS!! When you're reaching your max height, remember to level out your board with your front foot. Give it a little push at the peak so it won't look like a rocket ollie.

Here's a slow motion ollie. NOTE how they give it a little nudge at the peak.

Go find more ollies, slow it down and notice the little push. That is what make the ollie look nice.

Practice

This will take a couple weeks to land your first ollie. After that just practice them going faster, over stuff etc. This will make them look better, but it will take couple months for them to look good, less if you get comfortable faster. Don't Quit!!

Fakie

MAIN TIP: Always remember to lean towards your board to get a good air time when popping.

Might feel weird at first but just do your normal ollie and lean towards your board. Not difficult unless you're really confortable on your board.

Nollie

Get confortable just giving small pops on your nose, as if you're trying to do an nollie. The most difficult part would be sliding your back foot. Sliding the foot is going to level it out for nollies.

Switch

Everything is the same for a switch ollie, the foot placement and the technique but in your switch position. Getting comfortable riding switch is the first step for this trick.
