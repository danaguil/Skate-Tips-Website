-- Pop Shove-It --

**We will have both FS and BS pop shove it in this page**

How To BS Pop Shove-It

This video explain perfectly on how to pop shove-it!!

Practice first stationary(not moving) before rolling in order to learn it quickly.

Practice

Before popping your board, try leaning a bit forward so gain that pop and add pressure as well. If the board flips instead of just rotating, adjust the pressure and angle of your scoop.

Practice popping the board and catching it with your front hand to get used to the timing and height needed to get it down faster.

Fakie

MAIN TIP: Can start by going very slow when doing your fakie pop shove-it then progress your speed through the days.

Get your confidence up and just like a regular shove it, pop and land. Will take timing to land, so relax, focus and land that trick.

Slow motion of the trick following with more helpful tips.

Nollie

Everything is on your popping foot, push your board to the direction you're riding, just like you're riding fakie. If nollie shove it is easy for you, just give it a pop and you'll land it.

Switch

Make sure you are popping your board and giving great control of your board, similar technique as your regular pop shove it, so practice and practice.

How To FS Pop Shove-It

*Very simlilar to FS Shove-It*

This video explain perfectly on how to FS shove-it!!

One of my favorite tricks to do because they will look nice if done properly.

Practice

Remember to jump backwards when popping. After you pop your front foot will catch first, following your backfoot. Tip: learn it on grass/carpet, less fear and easier to land one. There's not much of a scoop in this trick, but a little pop, could be tricky, but unless you give a little pop and really give it a shove, it's look good

MAIN TIP: If you have you front foot hanging out a bit more and in the middle, then you will get a bigger pop. You can increase the height by how lower you have your front foot. Test where your foot needs to be.

Fakie

One can give a little scoop with Fakie FS Shove and I will say it way easier than regular FS Shove it. You can make it look 'lazy' if you're good at fakie.

This trick can be very easy than regular FS Shove for some people, so experiment and see what works for you!!

Nollie

Same technique as a FS Shove it and fakie FS Shove it, you just really gotta focus on that shove motion to get it's full rotation and remember to jump back and commmit.

Switch

Going to be a bit tricky to get this one good, but as long you give it a good pop you'll be good to go.
