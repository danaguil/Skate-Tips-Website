Knowing Your Foot Position

If you don't know your foot position then 1) Get anybody to stand behind you. 2) Have them push you. 3) Then foot you catch your self will be your front foot on the board

Here's a video that has more on foot placement/stance

Getting Comfortable

Great you found your stance. Here's the SECRET to be comfortable with your board. RIDE AROUND EVERYDAY. Literally go to a empty parking lot,park,front-yard, or anywhere with smooth ground and just ride around.

If you got that down, go find small hills or bumps to catch speed. You'll learn how to monuver and stop smoothly. That's how you'll get better quick!! Consider this as a warm-up before your skating journey

What If I Get Hurt?

That's what Skateboarding is about. Sorry. Everyone will catch that pebble on the ground or miss that landing and end up hurting their arm or shoulder. EVEN PROS GET INJURED.

Have someone with you at all time to keep an eye on you or someone to grab onto. Doesn't hurt to have someone help you out.

What's Riding Fakie?

Definition: Going backward. Here's a video showing fakie.

If you're still confused here what to do. Get on your normal stance. Then have someone push you, not front the back, but this time from the front. Then look where your going. That's riding fakie. Might feel weird but it's going to unlock so many tricks!!

What's Riding Nollie?

Get on your normal stance. Then have your front foot on the nose(top) of the board then your back foot in the middle.

What's Riding Switch

Opposite in which you are riding in. Extremely difficult to get confortable so it's best to practice riding switch and doing basic monuvers, then you can do a switch ollie etc.
